Who is Boris Izzaguirre?

Boris Rodolfo Izaguirre Lobo (Caracas, 29 September 1965), better known as Boris Izaguirre, is a Venezuelan-Spanish writer, TV host, screenwriter, journalist, and journalist showman.
Since 2007, the English Wikipedia page of Boris Izaguirre has received more than 61,474 page views. His biography is available in 20 different languages on Wikipedia. Boris Izaguirre is the 5,308th most popular writer (down from 4,611th in 2019), the 84th most popular biography from Venezuela, and the 6th most popular Venezuelan writer.
