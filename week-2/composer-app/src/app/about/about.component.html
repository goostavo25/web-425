Astolfo Romero


Astolfo Romero was born on February 8, 1950, in "El Empedrao" in Maracaibo, Venezuela. He was considered a child prodigy, playing several Zulia music instruments, including furro, drums, maracas, and charrasca. In 1962, at the age of 12, he joined a child piper group called “Los Invasores”. By then, he had already composed a piece ("Zulia") that was featured on the regional television channel. He continued his passion by composing music for different musical groups throughout his life until he passed away in 2000.


Works:

  • La Negra Cumba Cumba, 1974
  • Una Florecita, 1983
  • La Flor de la Habana, 1992
